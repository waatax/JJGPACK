Our Story

About JJG Paper Bag

Juang Jia Guoo Co., Ltd. is a professional paper bag manufacturer in Taiwan with over 70 years of production experience, specializing in the development and manufacturing of various food paper bags and commercial paper bags.

We provide greaseproof paper bags, laminated paper bags, kraft paper bags, white kraft paper bags, handle paper bags, shopping bags, and custom-printed paper bags, serving the needs of restaurants, chain brands, food factories, retail channels, and a wide range of corporate clients.

With "quality, safety, and reliable supply" as our core values, our products comply with food safety standards and international certifications, ensuring stable production capacity, large-volume order support, and dependable delivery schedules.
